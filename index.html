    IIIT BHOPAL


    FOUNDATION

    The IT industry is developing at an exponential rate. To keep up with this rapidly increasing change in the IT industry, IIIT-Bhopal was established.

    IIIT-Bhopal is striving to make a difference in the way in students, so that they are ready to create, innovate and lead in the IT industry as soon as they graduate.

    DAILY LIFE

    At IIIT-Bhopal, students get to rediscover themselves, in terms of their personal values, strengths and aspirations.

    A normal IIIT-Bhopal student remains engrossed with lectures, workshops, seminars, exercises, and mini-projects.
